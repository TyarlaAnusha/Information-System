TYPES OF INORGANIC FERTILIZERS

Nitrogenous fertilizers

The nitrogen-containing organic substances that supply the nutrition of nitrogen to the plants.They are of four types:

  • Nitrate:e.g.Sodium nitrate.
  • Nitrate and Ammonium:e.g.Ammonium Sulphate.
  • Amide: e.g.Urea.

    • Phosphatic Fertilizers:

    Phosphatic fertilizers are the phosphorous-containing organic substances that supply the nutrition of phosphorous to the plants.

  • Calcium dihydrogen phosphate,
  • Ammonium phosphate
  • Ammonium hydrogen phosphate
  • Super phosphate

    • Potassium Fertilizers:

    Potassium fertilizers are the potassium-containing organic substances that supply the nutrition of potassium to the plants.

  • Potassium chloride
  • Potassium sulphate
  • Potassium nitrate
  • kainite
